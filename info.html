CRICKET - IPL

Cricket IPL, also known as the Indian Premier League, is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India. It is one of the most popular and prestigious cricket tournaments in the world. The IPL was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007 and the inaugural season took place in 2008. The IPL follows a franchise-based model, where teams representing different cities in India compete against each other. Currently, there are 10 teams in the IPL: Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB), Chennai Super Kings (CSK), Delhi Capitals (DC), Kings XI Punjab (KXIP, now known as Punjab Kings), Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR), Mumbani Indians (MI), Rajasthan Royals (RR), Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH), Gujarat gaints, Lucknow, The IPL season usually takes place between March and May each year, although the exact dates can vary. Matches are played in a round-robin format, followed by playoffs, which culminate in the final to determine the champion team of that season.
